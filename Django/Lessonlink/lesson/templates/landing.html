About LessonLink

LessonLink was created by educators, for educators. Our mission is to empower Filipino teachers with technology that enhances their teaching practice while respecting their expertise and the unique needs of Filipino students.

Developed in partnership with the WMSU College of Teacher Education in Zamboanga City, our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with deep pedagogical understanding of the Philippine educational context.
