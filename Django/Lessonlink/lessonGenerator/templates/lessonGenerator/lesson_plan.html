Lesson Information

Mathematics

Grade 5

45 minutes

Learning Objectives

  • Students will understand the fundamental concept of fractions and their real-world applications
  • Students will be able to identify and create equivalent fractions using visual models
  • Students will solve basic fraction problems with confidence and accuracy

Required Materials

  • Fraction circles and manipulatives
  • Interactive whiteboard and markers
  • Student activity worksheets
  • Calculators (optional support)

Lesson Activities

Introduction & Warm-up

10 minutes

Begin with a review of whole numbers and engage students with visual fraction demonstrations. Use real-world examples like pizza slices to introduce fraction concepts naturally.

Main Learning Activity

25 minutes

Interactive hands-on exploration using fraction circles, collaborative group work to discover equivalent fractions, and guided practice with increasing complexity.

Conclusion & Assessment

10 minutes

Comprehensive review of key concepts, student reflections on learning, and assignment of purposeful homework to reinforce understanding.

Assessment & Evaluation

Formative Assessment: Continuous observation during collaborative activities, real-time feedback during hands-on manipulation, and targeted questioning to gauge understanding.

Summative Assessment: Exit ticket featuring three carefully designed fraction problems that assess comprehension at different cognitive levels.
